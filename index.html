Hello, I'm Lynn, and Design is my passion.

Armed with a camera, sketchbook, pen, curiosity, and open-mind, I approach problems through a design-thinking point of view. Design is everywhere, but good design takes thorough observation, analysis, and understanding of a particular task and user.

I've worked on a wide variety of design problems, from web tools to mobile applications, from wayfinding to classic product design. See my projects here. I believe in closely studying and interacting with situations and user groups to inform effective and usable designs.

But art and research go hand-in-hand. Sketching provides unique views and perspectives of interactions (see my work with KidPrint) and allows clear thinking and creativity. Digital visualization is often the most effective way to communicate design ideas. See my art here. With these tools, I'm eager to tackle any problem and produce novel, exciting design solutions.
